Authorize Copilot Pro in your Github Account

As a student with a .ocadu email address you have access to Copilot Pro for free. This provides more features and capacity over the free account. You can see the differences here (https://docs.github.com/en/copilot/get-started/plans) .

They have recently changed the authorization process and added more steps, so follow them below.

Required

  • Your Student ID
  • Github account you are logged in to

Summary

What You'll Enable

  • GitHub Copilot Pro features
  • Enhanced AI code assistance
  • Increased usage limits
  • Advanced code suggestions

Account Requirements

  • Two-factor authentication enabled
  • Billing information updated
  • Profile name matching student ID
  • OCADU student email verified

Authorization Process

  • GitHub Education benefits verification
  • Student status confirmation
  • Automatic Copilot Pro activation

Part 1 - Set up Your Profile

To authorize your account, you are required to make updates to your Github account.

Step 1 - Turn on 2 Factor Authentication

If you haven't already, you need to turn on at least 1 method of 2FA for your account

  • Go to https://github.com/settings/security
  • Scroll down to Two Factor Authentication and ensure at least 1 is configured

Step 2 - Update Your Billing Information

You are NOT required to add a credit card to your account, but your name in your Billing information must match the name on your OCADU student ID exactly. If you have only a single legal name, enter it in both the first and last name fields.

  • Go to https://github.com/settings/billing/payment_information
  • Under Billing Information, hit edit and enter your Name exactly as it appears on your ID

Step 3 - Update Your Public Profile

You also need to update your Name in your public profile so that it matches your ID exactly.

  • Go to https://github.com/
  • Click your profile icon in the top right
  • Select Profile
  • Click the Edit Profile button
  • Update your name
  • Press Save

Part 2 - Authorizing your account

After updating your account, you will need to authorize it using your OCAD ID. If the process fails:

  • Check that the information is correct from Part 1
  • Log out and Log back in to your account
  • Try again
